enum CppRayTracerChallenge::Core::Renderer::CSG::Operation
strong

Opereration that a CSG takes on its left and right child shapes

Enumerator
UNION 

Union combines inputs into a single shape, preserving all external surfaces.

INTERSECT 

Intersection preserves the portion of the inputs that share a volume, resulting in a single shape with those combined surfaces.

DIFFERENCE 

Difference preserves only the portion of the first shape where it's not overlapped by the others.
